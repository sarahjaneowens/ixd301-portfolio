About Me

Hi there, My name is Sarah Jane! I'm a 21 year old coffee drinking graphic | web designer based in Belfast, NI. I am currently studying Interaction Design at Ulster University. I have a great passion for learning new techniques and also the new ways of technology.

During my degree so far, I have gained a wide range of design skills and knowledge. It has broadened my mind, allowing me to appreicate the different aspects that revolve around design, that I never really acknowledged before. I now see design in everything! 'Design wont save the world, but it damn sure makes it look good.'

What I Can Do?

Web Design

Creating a modern fresh website, I can help. Using my knowledge of HTML & CSS, combined with my design skills allows me to create and develop websites.

Concept Development

For any of my projects I always start on paper. Paper prototyping allows me to communicate my ideas into something visual quick and easy, even before any design or coding has been implemented.

Graphic Design

My skill to use different programs such as Illustrator, Sketch and Photoshop, allows me to create illustrations and icons with my own style. Not only that I can also help with Branding, advertising, business cards & more.

My Skills

On the digital side, using HTML5, CSS3,Javascript, and Illustrator, I can create small static sites to a basic multipage site. I build sites that don't just work and look good on a laptop or Pc, but also works perfectly fine on other devices such as a tablet or mobile phone.
